Tap to the Beat. Fight Elemental Bosses. Ascend.

TapHorizon: Ascend is a rhythm-action game built for players who love the rush of nailing every beat. Notes fall down tap lanes in time with the music -- hit them with precision to build combos, rack up points, and unleash elemental powers.

Five elemental realms, each with its own original soundtrack and visual style. From chill 80 BPM grooves in the Earth Realm to relentless 140 BPM onslaughts in the Celestial Realm. Patterns grow more complex as you ascend.

Chain perfect hits to build multipliers up to 5x, then face epic boss battles at the end of each realm. Defeat all five guardians across 50 handcrafted levels to reach full ascension.
